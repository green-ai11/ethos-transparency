# Sample training data

This directory will contain **50–100 representative examples** from the Ethos-32B training corpus — enough to illustrate format, tone, and review standards without exposing the full dataset.

---

## Current status

**Empty by design.** We are not publishing placeholder or fabricated examples. When committee-reviewed samples are ready, they will appear here as documented, redacted rows (format TBD — likely JSONL with schema described in this README).

---

## Full corpus

The complete public dataset is planned for Hugging Face:

- **Dataset name (planned):** `ethos-training-data-v1`
- **Publisher:** SustainAI Global
- **Status:** Not published yet

Until the Hugging Face dataset is live, refer to [ETHICAL_GUIDELINES.md](../../ETHICAL_GUIDELINES.md) and [MODEL_CARD.md](../../MODEL_CARD.md) for data philosophy and scale (approximately 8k–25k curated examples).

---

## What samples will demonstrate

When published, representative rows will illustrate:

- Instruction/response structure used in supervised fine-tuning
- Alignment with [CHARTER.md](../../CHARTER.md) principles (humility, truth, stewardship, service, and related themes)
- Jobs-first and systems-thinking framing in sustainable economics and transition topics
- Committee review criteria (summarized in file headers or companion docs)

Samples are **illustrative**, not statistically representative of every topic weight in training.
