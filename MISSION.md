# SustainAI Global — Mission

SustainAI Global is a Texas nonprofit corporation (501(c)(3) pending) building AI for the **public good**. Our mission is to advance ethical, specialized intelligence that strengthens economies, protects the planet, and expands opportunity for people — not to concentrate power in a few platforms or accelerate a jobless future.

Learn more and get involved at [sustainai.global](https://sustainai.global/).

---

## Mission pillars

### Public good first

We prioritize outcomes that benefit communities, workers, and ecosystems. Transparency, accountability, and open documentation (including this repository) are part of how we earn trust.

### Jobs-first

Technology should **create and sustain meaningful work**, not merely automate it away. We design models and programs that support employment, skills, and fair transitions — especially in energy, manufacturing, and green industries.

### Truly green

“Green” means measurable environmental integrity: renewable-powered development where possible, honest accounting of impacts, and systems thinking across supply chains — not greenwashing or vague sustainability claims.

### Humans and AI flourishing together

We pursue **Ethical Specialized Intelligence (ESI)**: AI that augments human judgment, defers when uncertain, and refuses harmful shortcuts — partnership with humanity, not replacement of it. ESI is grounded in [The Charter for Beneficial Intelligence](CHARTER.md) and its Ten Principles.

### Non-partisan, pro-business

We work across political perspectives with a practical bias: thriving businesses, stable communities, and evidence-based policy. We engage employers, workers, educators, and civic leaders as allies in a just transition.

## Green Points program

**Green Points** is SustainAI’s community recognition program on [sustainai.global](https://sustainai.global/). Members earn points through participation, learning, and contributions aligned with our mission. **Tier 3 (Green Steward) and above** receive early hosted **chat** access (see [USAGE.md](USAGE.md)); the full five-tier program is described on the website.

The program is designed to reward constructive engagement with sustainable economics and ethical AI — not speculation or extractive behavior. Details and tier benefits are maintained on the website.

---

## What this mission means for Ethos-32B

Ethos-32B is our first flagship ESI model. It embodies this mission in its training focus (sustainable economics, renewable energy, green finance, just transition) and in how we ship it: transparent documentation here, committee-reviewed data philosophy, and **hosted-only** access so we can steward safety and fairness at the edge.
