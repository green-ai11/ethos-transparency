# Vision — Ethical Specialized Intelligence for a thriving future

SustainAI Global envisions a world where AI **unlocks human potential** — where specialized models help people see systems clearly, make better collective choices, and build economies that are prosperous, equitable, and ecologically sound.

We refuse a **jobless future** as inevitable. We refuse AI that optimizes only for engagement or extraction. We build **Ethical Specialized Intelligence (ESI)** in **partnership with humanity**: tools that know their limits, defer to experts, and strengthen institutions rather than undermine them.

Explore our work at [sustainai.global](https://sustainai.global/).

---

## AI that unlocks human potential

General intelligence scattered across ad platforms has not delivered broad prosperity. We believe the next wave is **specialized, values-aligned models** — like Ethos-32B — that help policymakers, entrepreneurs, educators, and communities reason about:

- Sustainable economics and circular systems
- Renewable energy deployment and grid transition
- Green finance, risk, and accountability
- Just transition for workers and regions

ESI is not “autopilot for society.” It is **decision support** guided by [The Charter for Beneficial Intelligence](CHARTER.md), with humility, traceable values, and public transparency about how models are built.

---

## Partnership, not replacement

Ethos is designed to:

- **Augment** research, planning, and education — not replace licensed professionals in law, medicine, or safety-critical engineering
- **Defer** when evidence is thin, stakes are high, or local context matters
- **Encourage** human agency, job creation, and community voice in outcomes

Our agent and hosted layers exist to route questions responsibly and keep humans in the loop for consequential choices.

---

## 100% renewable by 2030

We commit to pushing our own AI development toward **100% renewable electricity by 2030**, with transparent reporting as we scale. Today, much of our alignment and iteration work runs on **solar-powered development hardware**; we are actively seeking **renewable GPU capacity** and partners who share this constraint as a feature, not an afterthought.

Energy honesty is part of ethical AI: models trained and served on fossil-heavy grids carry a hidden footprint. We want Ethos to stand for the transition it discusses.

---

## Transparency as strategy

This repository is one face of that vision: public methodology, public model card philosophy, and eventual public training data on Hugging Face — while weights stay hosted so we can protect users and fulfill nonprofit stewardship.
